ISO 9001-2000 Certification

We have been recognized as a leading manufacturer and supplier of Domestic De-Fluoridation Filters in Andhra Pradesh, catering to fluoride-affected districts such as Prakasam, Kurnool, Warangal, and Karimnagar. Our organization has got the ISO 9001-2000 Certification, and the filters are manufactured following guidelines from UNICEF and I.I.T. Kanpur. Product quality has been inspected by district officials S.E‘s RWS, including the RWS and Panchayat Raj departments of Andhra Pradesh.
